What should the notebook page look like?

The background texture sets the whole mood of the editor.

A — Classic ruled paper

Warm off-white background, horizontal blue lines, red left margin. Like a school notebook.

B — Dot grid

Subtle dot matrix background. Modern and minimal — like a Leuchtturm bullet journal.

C — Clean white with binding shadow

Pure white page, no lines, subtle left-edge shadow suggesting a bound book. Elegant and distraction-free.
